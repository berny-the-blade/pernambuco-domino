Privacy Policy

Domino Pernambucano — Last updated: March 10, 2025

🔒 Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Domino Pernambucano is a free, casual domino game for Android. It is developed and maintained independently. The game is available at berny-the-blade.github.io/pernambuco-domino.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or gameplay statistics
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Payment information

3. How the Game Works

Domino Pernambucano runs entirely on your device and in your browser. The AI opponent is powered by a neural network model that is downloaded once and runs locally — no game data is sent to any server.

Online multiplayer features (if enabled) use Firebase Realtime Database solely to relay game moves between players in real time. No personal information is stored — only temporary, anonymous game state data that is deleted when the session ends.

4. Third-Party Services

The game may load resources from:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Firebase — for real-time multiplayer relay only (no personal data stored)

We do not use Google Analytics, AdMob, or any advertising SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13, or from anyone at all.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
